Peanut Butter and Jelly

A.K.A. PBJ

Something fruity, something fatty, and something to spread it on ~ Chef, Justin Warner

The National Peanut Board describes it as: comforting, delicious, power packed, versatile and convenient, and fast and affordable!

Needed Ingredients:

  • Bomb-ass Bread
  • Heavenly Peanut Butter
  • Titllating Jam

Directions

  1. Lay out two pieces of bread
  2. Take the lids of the jars
  3. Use a knife to scoop some PB on one piece of bread
  4. After making sure the knife is clean, use it to scoop and spead some jam on the other piece of bread
  5. Put the two pieces of bread together, making sure that the PB and jam are touching eachother
  6. Cut the sandwhich in half vertically, horizantally, or diagonally. Chef's choice!!!

Sit back, relax, and enjoy!!!
